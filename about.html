Who is Dev?

Dev Patel is currently a junior at the Indiana University‐Bloomington, where he is currently studying Data Science. He is a first-generation college student, and he takes great pride in pursuing a higher education and building a future that reflects both his personal goals and his family's sacrifices.

In the beginning of his life journey, it has been shaped by a unique cultural background, being that he spent his early childhood in India before his family moved to the United States when he was at the age of eleven. By growing up in two different environments, this allowed Dev to experience diverse perspectives, but it presented a challenge of adapting to a new language and a different life-style.

One of the most meaningful obstacles Dev overcame was the language barrier he faced after relocating to the United States. Yet, through dedication and persistence, he did not only adapt successfully but also went on to learn and speak up to three other languages. His accomplishment and determination reflects his dependable and goal-oriented personality, as well as his ability to connect with people from many different backgrounds.

Dev credits much of his daily motivation to his parents, who made significant life changes to provide greater opportunities for him and his family.Outside of academics, Dev enjoys spending his free time playing video games with friends and engaging in motorsport-related hobbies.

He has a strong passion for cars and racing, which has grown over the years through friendships and personal interest, including time spent using a racing simulator. Dev is also committed to staying active and frequently goes to the gym.

Looking ahead, Dev is focused on securing a summer internship in a data science-related field and hopes to begin his career as a data scientist or analyst after graduation.
